## Abstract

The purpose of this paper is to introduce the transfer principle and its model theoretic consequences in nonstandard analysis. Starting from fundamental ideas about nonstandard analysis, we analyze the properties behind the theory of the hyperreals in order to build justification for the transfer principle. Eventually, we develop several perspectives on the transfer principle and provide explicit constructions that satisfy the transfer principle. Notably, we develop the theory of ultrapowers and show how it serves as a model for the hyperreals. We also prove the famous Łoś Theorem, which is the mathematical certification that ultrapower models satisfy the transfer principle.
